Customer information

Ordering, Delivery & Safety

Helpful information about ordering from Shine Without Limits, including payment, custom orders, delivery, returns and product safety notes.

How ordering works

Orders are currently handled by email so every product and custom request can be checked properly before payment.

  • Choose a product or build a PECS card bundle.
  • Send your order request by email.
  • We confirm the details, price, postage and timescale.
  • Payment details are sent after everything is agreed.

Payment

Payment is confirmed after your order details have been checked. This is especially useful for custom PECS cards, personalised bundles and made-to-order products.

  • PayPal payment option available.
  • Final total confirmed before payment.
  • No custom order is started until details are agreed.

Delivery

Delivery times can vary depending on the product, quantity and whether the order is personalised.

  • Estimated timescale confirmed by email.
  • Postage cost confirmed before payment.
  • Custom orders may take longer than ready-made items.

Returns & custom items

If there is a problem with your order, please get in touch so it can be looked into.

  • Ready-made items may be returnable if unused and in suitable condition.
  • Personalised or custom-made items may not be returnable unless faulty.
  • Any issue should be reported as soon as possible after delivery.

Product safety

Some sensory items and small products may contain small parts or be unsuitable for unsupervised use.

  • Adult supervision is recommended where appropriate.
  • Products are not intended to be chewed unless clearly stated.
  • Check items regularly and stop use if damaged.
  • Colours and finish may vary slightly on handmade or 3D printed items.

Custom PECS cards

Custom PECS cards can be made for routines, emotions, places, food choices, school needs, days out, toilet training and family-specific communication.

  • Choose from ready-made options.
  • Add your own words or ideas.
  • Build your own bundle using the PECS card builder.

Need help before ordering?
