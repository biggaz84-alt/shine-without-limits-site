Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order PECS cards?

Click Build Your Set on any PECS product. You can choose your cards and the basket will apply the best available price automatically.

🎁

Do bundle discounts apply?

Yes. Bundle discounts are applied automatically when they work out cheaper, including 10-card, 20-card and 40-card bundle pricing.

🎨

Can I request custom cards?

Yes. Custom PECS cards can be made for routines, places, emotions, food choices, school needs and family life. Photo-based cards are not available at the moment.

📦

How long does an order take?

Times can vary depending on the product and custom details. We’ll confirm timescale, postage and payment by email.
